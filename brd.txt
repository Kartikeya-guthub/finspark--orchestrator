Project FastTrack: Integration Requirements v1.2Overview:We are launching a new "Instant Personal Loan" product. To make this work, we need to stitch together a few providers. The main goal is to automate the journey so a customer gets money in their account without us manually checking spreadsheets.1. Identity & Onboarding:First things first, we can't give money to ghosts. Every single person who applies must go through the UIDAI Aadhaar eKYC flow (we’re using the v2.0 API). I need the name, DOB, and that OTP verification step to be solid. If the KYC fails or the person cancels mid-way, just stop the whole process right there. No point in spending money on bureau hits.2. The Credit Check Stuff:Once we know who they are, we need to see if they actually pay their bills. Pull a CIBIL score for everyone. Now, here is a special rule from the Risk Team: If the applicant is asking for more than 5,00,000 INR, we need a second opinion. In those high-value cases, please also hit the Experian bureau. If it's a small loan, just CIBIL is fine.3. Safety First:Before we actually send any cash, run a quick fraud check. We've got that FraudShield tool (internal v2.0). It needs the IP address and phone number from the application. We should only do this after the credit bureaus say they are okay.4. Show Me The Money:For the final step, we’re using Razorpay. Once the fraud check gives us the green light and the manager's internal status is set to 'approved', go ahead and trigger the disbursal.
